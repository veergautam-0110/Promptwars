# Climora: Carbon Intelligence Orchestration

Climora is a world-class carbon footprint tracker and sustainability Eco coach designed to help users understand, measure, and reduce their environmental impact through precision data and collective intelligence.

## 🌍 Chosen Vertical
**Climate Technology & Sustainability (Ecotech)**
Climora addresses the "Green India Tracker" initiative, focusing on India's goal to reach Net Zero by 2070. It serves as a personal and community-level command center for environmental stewardship.

## 🧠 Approach and Logic
The application follows a **"Precision-Impact"** philosophy:
- **High-Performance UI**: Utilizing a high-contrast "Cosmic" theme (Black & Neon Lime) to shift the sustainability narrative from "passive green" to "active intelligence."
- **Modular Data Handling**: Logic is divided into specialized modules for transport, diet, and energy, ensuring that carbon calculations remain granular and actionable.
- **Social Syncing**: The architecture prioritizes community data, showing users that their individual savings contribute to a larger regional "Pulse."

## 🚀 How the Solution Works
1. **Measurement (Neural Link)**: Users input their lifestyle data into the **Green India Tracker** (Calculator). The system converts these inputs into kg CO2e metrics.
2. **Visualization (Command Center)**: The **Dashboard** uses advanced charting to show impact trends over multiple time horizons (weekly to yearly).
3. **Education (Academy)**: Users expand their "eco-consciousness" through structured knowledge modules.
4. **Action (AI Coach)**: personalized "Challenges" (Initiation Protocols) provide specific, high-impact tasks to reduce footprints.
5. **Collection (Collective Intelligence)**: The **Standings** and **Eco Hubs** allow users to sync with regional nodes and compete for the top spot in India's sustainability rankings.

## 🛠️ Assumptions Made
- **Emission Factors**: Calculations are based on generalized industry-standard CO2e coefficients for the Indian region.
- **Persistence Strategy**: "Guest Protocol" uses client-side state/localStorage for immediate utility, while "Authenticated Protocol" uses Firebase Firestore for durable cloud synchronization.
- **Geospatial Data**: Regional savings and live community clusters are simulated for the prototype to demonstrate the potential of Google Maps Platform integration.
- **Impact Scaling**: Total savings are calculated relative to regional benchmarks to provide meaningful context to the user's status.
